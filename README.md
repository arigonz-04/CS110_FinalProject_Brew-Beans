# Brew & Beans Project

## Description

This project is going to contain resemblance to Facebook Marketplace but is specifically going to be targeted towards coffee users.

Sellers register on the system and create listings for coffee-related items they want to sell.

Buyers register on the system and search for coffee products that match their interests or what they’re specifically looking for.

Sellers provide descriptions, pricing, product images, categories, and product condition in their listing
Buyers are able to look through multiple listings, look at the different product images, search for a specific product, talk with sellers through a messaging system, and leave ratings and reviews after a transaction has been made.

Users can customize their profiles by adding name, selling history, and a short message about themselves.

The system gives recommendations based on a user’s interests (through browsing activity and previously viewed products).

Ratings and reviews help users see who is trustworthy and sells quality products.
